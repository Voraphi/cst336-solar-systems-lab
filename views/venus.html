2nd planet in our Solar System, second closest to our sun. A moonless planet which rotates in the opposite direction than earth. The sun would rise in the west and set in the east. Its year would be ~2/3 of earths year, 225 earth days. Having the slowest rotation it takes longer to rotate about its axis than its orbit around the sun, 243 earth days.
